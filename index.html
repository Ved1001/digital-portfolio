About Me

Hello! I'm Ved Shrimali, a passionate Java Backend Developer specializing in Spring Boot and microservices architecture. Currently pursuing B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering at Pandit Deendayal Energy University (PDEU), Gandhinagar.
